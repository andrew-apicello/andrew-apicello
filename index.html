Andrew Apicello's About Me:

Discovery exhilarates me. In pursuit of unearthing new patterns and ideas, I have studied science, business, martial arts, music, and engineering. It strikes me that in the moment of understanding even a simple concept, we catch a glimpse of a singular truth. I find myself enthralled in wonder of my surroudings, both physical and philosophical, and hope to spend my whole life exporing this amazing place we call home.

Many times, we skip over the most important aspects of each other when introducing ourselves. I wonder how many fantastic people I pass by each day, never knowing their story. This page is for anyone who wants to get to know me a little better. I hope you stay awhile, check out some links, and get to enjoy a beautiful thought today!
